Coworking Terms and Member Agreement

Membership

Start Charlotte coworking membership does not creates a tenancy, but a prepaid usage license to use the provided amenities on a monthly basis. This license may be cancelled by either Start Charlotte or the licensee at any time.

Member Conduct

All members shall adhere to the rules of conduct at all times. Failure to do so will result in a stern talking-to, and possible revocation of coworking privileges.

These rules can be summarized as follows: Treat your fellow coworkers with respect. If you need specifics, they follow:

Quiet Enjoyment

All members and guests have the right to quiet enjoyment and any behavior that breaches this will not be condoned.

Open Environment

Start Charlotte Coworking is an open space shared by many different people and organizations. Don't say or do anything you're not comfortable with everyone seeing, or that may make your coworkers uncomfortable.

Internet / Network Usage

Be respectful of the bandwidth. No spam, no illegal behavior. Start Charlotte will not monitor the network unless we have to.

No Pets or Bikes

No pets of any kind. Bikes can be stored to the left of the main entrance on the bike rack.

Member Responsibilities

Leave your desk in better shape than you found it. Be nice to your fellow coworkers. Pay your bills. That is all.

Not So Fine Print

Coworking membership, and the terms of that membership are subject to change pending 30 day notification of the membership. This includes these terms, coworking rates, and facility policies.

Deposit

No deposit is required to license the Start Charlotte coworking facilities.

Payment

Payment is due for each month's license, in advance, based on the date and license rate you started with. An interruption of license will reset the license rate to the current rate. Should the license rate ever decrease, all members will be reduced to the new rate on their next billing statement.

Keys

Some members may be issued keys to the facilities. These keys remain the property of Start Charlotte, LLC, and must be returned before a membership is cancelled. A bond may be assessed before keys are issued; if so, that bond will be remitted when the keys are returned.

Liability

As a member, you agree to hold Start Charlotte, LLC blameless for any injury or damage suffered by you or your equipment while on the premises. Conversely, you are responsible for any damage done to the property of Start Charlotte, LLC, or any other member while present at the coworking facility.

Renewal and Termination

Renewal is a privilege, not a right. All management decisions are final, and all payments are non-refundable.

Conflicts with Other Members

This isn't high school. Work it out like adults.

Finally

This space is provided with the understanding that scheduled events, environmental effects, and decisions by the management of Area 15 may affect your ability to use the space. There are no guarantees of any kind concerning available hours, resources, or facilities.

We all do the best we can. Enjoy.
