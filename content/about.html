About Start Charlotte

"In the middle of difficulty lies opportunity." —Einstein

Start Charlotte is a collection of entrepreneurs, freelance consultants, designers, and software developers committed to the idea that given enough support, Charlotte can become a haven for startup development.

We are committed to continuing the conversation, promoting and discussing startup culture, and helping the freelance and entrepreneurial community in Charlotte, NC mature into a cohesive whole.

For a more details, read our mission detail.
